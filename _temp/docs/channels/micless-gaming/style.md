# Style Guide — MicLessGaming

## Format
- Super-cuts of gameplay — no voiceover, no intro, no outro
- Starts and ends abruptly — just the moments

## Tone
- None — the gameplay speaks for itself
- No personality injection, no branding, pure gameplay

## Editing
- Lighter editing than Witty Gaming
- Cut around voiceover cues, then strip the voiceover track
- Keep the best gameplay moments, remove dead time
